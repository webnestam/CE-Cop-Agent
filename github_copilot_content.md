## Blog: Three Simple Steps to Never Miss a CE Renewal

If you've ever rushed to finish continuing education hours at the last minute, you know how stressful renewal season can be. The key to staying compliant without the last-minute scramble is to build a system. Follow these three steps and you'll never miss a CE renewal again.

### 1. Know your requirements

Every state and profession has different rules. Don't assume your renewal date or credit requirements are the same as your colleague's. Visit your state licensing board's website and write down your renewal date, the total hours of CE required and any specific course topics (for example, ethics or safety) you must complete. Keeping this information handy prevents surprises later on.

### 2. Centralise your records

Too many professionals use a mix of emails, paper certificates and sticky notes to track their CE hours. This scattershot approach leads to errors. Instead, create a single source of truth such as a spreadsheet with columns for course name, provider, date completed and hours earned. A unified record makes it easy to see your progress at a glance.

### 3. Automate your reminders

The final step is turning your spreadsheet into an active system. Automation ensures you never forget a deadline. Here's how our CE Compliance Agent helps:

- **Scheduled reminders:** It monitors your renewal date and sends emails 90, 60 and 30 days before deadlines.
- **Weekly summaries:** You receive a concise report every week outlining what you've completed and what's left.
- **Automatic calculations:** The agent tallies your CE hours and alerts you if you're falling short.

By automating reminders, you eliminate the risk of missing deadlines and free up mental space to focus on your clients.

### Ready to simplify your renewal?

You don't need to hire staff or invest in complex software. A straightforward, automated solution can handle all the details for you. Start by centralising your data and then let our CE Compliance Agent do the heavy lifting. You'll spend less time tracking hours and more time serving clients while staying fully compliant.

### Targeted communities

- **AppraisersForum.com:** Premier online community for real estate appraisers. Users discuss continuing education courses and share marketing tips.
- **InterNACHI Forum:** Large forum for home inspectors with categories on general inspection, specific topics, education and vendor services. There's a section where software and service providers can post.

### Posting tips

1. **Choose the right category:** On AppraisersForum, post in the Marketing or Appraisal Education sections. On InterNACHI, use the Vendors section or a relevant education topic.
2. **Avoid spam:** Write a genuine introduction, share your personal experience and invite feedback. Be transparent about your affiliation and pricing.
3. **Respond to comments:** If users ask questions about licensing requirements or features, reply promptly. This builds trust and keeps your post active.

While you can't fully automate forum posts (most require CAPTCHAs and manual verification), you can schedule reminders for yourself. Create a simple content calendar and plan to post or comment in two communities per week. Rotate between forums to avoid spamming one place. Use your CE tracking sheet to add a column for outreach tasks; your agent can send you reminders to make a post or follow up. Finally, as part of your sign-up form, offer a free checklist and follow up automatically using a free email tool. This keeps leads warm without manual effort.
