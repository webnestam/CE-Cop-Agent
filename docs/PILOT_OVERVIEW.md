# CE-Cop Agent Pilot Overview

CE-Cop Agent is being prepared for founder-led pilots with real estate teams that already have inbound lead flow.

## Pilot Goal

The goal of a pilot is to prove that faster first response and cleaner qualification can reduce missed opportunities from website, social, and portal leads.

## Ideal Pilot Customer

- real estate team with 3 or more agents
- active listings or recent transactions
- website forms, social DMs, or paid lead sources
- clear team lead, broker owner, or operations contact
- willingness to review lead handoff quality each week

## Pilot Workflow

1. Map current lead sources and response process.
2. Identify where leads wait too long or get lost.
3. Configure the qualification questions and routing rules.
4. Run a small controlled workflow.
5. Review lead summaries, response speed, and handoff quality.

## Success Signals

- faster first response
- fewer unworked leads
- clearer buyer/seller intent before handoff
- easier follow-up for the team
- enough value to justify a monthly subscription

## Not In Scope

- legal advice
- brokerage compliance replacement
- guaranteed transaction volume
- handling sensitive financial or identity documents
