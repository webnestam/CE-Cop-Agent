# Lead Response Audit

The Lead Response Audit is a lightweight discovery asset for real estate teams evaluating CE-Cop Agent.

## What The Audit Reviews

- where inbound leads come from
- how quickly each lead source gets a response
- whether after-hours leads are handled
- whether buyer and seller intent is captured
- whether leads are routed to the correct person
- whether follow-up tasks are visible

## Questions To Ask

1. What happens when a website lead comes in after 7 p.m.?
2. Who owns Instagram, Facebook, Zillow, Realtor.com, and website inquiries?
3. How are buyer leads separated from seller valuation leads?
4. What lead source is most expensive right now?
5. Where do leads most often get delayed or lost?

## Output

The audit should produce a short recommendation:

- current risk
- missed-response points
- suggested automation workflow
- pilot scope
- next action

## Positioning

Use plain language:

> You already paid for the lead. CE-Cop Agent helps make sure someone follows up fast enough.
